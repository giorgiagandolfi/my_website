Data Availability

All datasets used in the SCOUT study are publicly available through repository archives. We distinguish between raw sequencing data and processed, analysis-ready datasets.

Raw Data

Raw whole-genome sequencing data generated in this study are available through the European Nucleotide Archive (ENA).

European Nucleotide Archive (ENA) WGS raw reads

Processed Data

Processed datasets, including SCOUT-derived outputs, simulation results, and downstream analysis artefacts, are deposited on Zenodo. Each SPN (Sample/Patient Node) has a dedicated Zenodo record.
